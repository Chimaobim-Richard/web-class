WHAT'S TIME???

Let's explore the dynamic thoughts surrounding the concept of time.

Time is the measurable period during which events and conditions are actualized. It is independent of all factors. Time is relative i.e; it causes change to other quantities. There are two perspectives of time. They are:

THE ORDINARY MAN'S PERSPECTIVE OF TIME.

As a layman, time is what determines a whole part of their lives. It is given for a living. The concept of time is understood by the layman as a means of exchange.

let's see how the businessman perceives time...

THE BUSINESSMAN'S PERSPECTIVE OF TIME.

Doesn't it bother you that the same 24 hours you've got is what another uses and makes his account rumble with joy.
Here is why:

TIME MADE THE AUXILIARY, NOT THE MAIN.

It is quite a common saying that time is money but what's the harm in trying Time+value= much money. Every person would love to go with the second option but the problem is "can they handle it???"

It is one thing to say "I'll do it" and another to actually do it.

CHARACTERISTICS OF A BUSINESSMAN.

  • The love for risk-taking.
  • The understanding of the principle of exchange,i.e give and take.
  • Knowing that big men handle big problems.
  • The "It's possible" mentality.

THE LOVE FOR RISK-TAKING

Businessmen see the pitfalls as opportunities. Rather than waiting for the gold to be refined, they move with the hope that it will work.

Even if it doesn't work, he/she feels satisfied for not sitting idle and making time the main factor for his/her success.

THE SIGNS OF EFFECTIVE USAGE OF TIME

  1. Slow but imminent increase in revenue.
  2. Temporary misery.
  3. A feeling of satisfaction at every moment.
